Nintendo TVii (JP)

Nintendo TVii was a Wii U applet that helped users discuss while watching TV and post to Miiverse. This version uses the Japanese TVii layout.

Miiverse was the social network of the Wii U and 3DS, letting players share messages, drawings, and screenshots.
